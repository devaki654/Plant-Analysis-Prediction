Our Mission

We aim to empower farmers and gardeners by providing a simple, efficient, and reliable tool for detecting plant diseases. This application uses advanced machine learning techniques to analyze leaf images and predict potential diseases, enabling timely intervention and better crop management.

How It Works

The application leverages a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on a diverse dataset of plant leaf images. By analyzing visual patterns, the model can accurately identify signs of diseases like rust, leaf spot, and powdery mildew.

Features

  • Real-time disease detection from leaf images.
  • Comprehensive database of plant diseases and symptoms.
  • Easy-to-use interface for farmers and gardeners.
